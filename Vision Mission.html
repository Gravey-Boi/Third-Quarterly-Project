Vision

Upholding the PUP Philosophy, the PUP Laboratory High School envisions to be a leading business secondary school which produces students with excellent academic foundation and positive moral values.

Mission

  • Provide Competitive, relevant, holistic and functional program that is responsive to the needs and challenges of society;

  • Develop globally competitive and socially responsible, research oriented, technologically adept, critical thinkers, and life-long learners;

  • Produce nationalistic, cultureoriented nation builders and Godfearing students with a deep sense of social responsibility and volunteerism; and

  • Inculcate planning and decision making skills and the importance of finding purpose, direction and meaning in the life of the LHSians.
